pill Overlay pill — Marshmallow neumorphic cushion · lilac/amber/blush accents · ✕ cancel · ↻ retry

The pill is a soft raised cushion — cream in light, plum in dark. Waveform colour carries the state: lilac while listening, amber while transcribing, gold while polishing. Toasts (done/cancelled/error) expand to fit their label and add a 6 s countdown bar. The ✕ cancel and ↻ retry buttons are 22 px ghost discs at the pill's right edge.
